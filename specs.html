Important Hardware Terms

To understand these specs, you need to know these key terms:

  • VRAM: Video Memory used specifically by your Graphics Card.
  • Clock Speed: How many cycles a CPU can perform per second (Measured in GHz).
  • Bottleneck: When one slow part prevents the fast parts from working at full speed.
