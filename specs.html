Hardware Requirements Guide

When choosing parts from the table above, it is important to ensure Compatibility. For example, a high-end RTX 4090 graphics card requires a large case and a very powerful Power Supply Unit.

For students of ICT, the Extreme Tier represents the cutting edge of semiconductor technology, allowing for real-time ray tracing and complex 3D simulations.


Important Hardware Terms:

  • Bottleneck: When one slow part stops a fast part from working at full speed.
  • Clock Speed: How fast a CPU can process instructions (measured in GHz).
  • VRAM: Video Memory used by the graphics card to store textures.
