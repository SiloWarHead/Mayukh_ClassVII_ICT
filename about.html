About Our Project

Learn more about the mission of Elite Gaming Builds.

1. The Website Mission

The goal of Elite Gaming Builds is to provide a clear and concise resource for individuals interested in custom PC assembly. In the modern world of ICT, understanding how computer hardware functions is essential for maximizing performance and efficiency.

This website acts as a bridge between high-end engineering and the end-user, offering insights into the components that make a computer powerful.


2. Why We Built This Site

  • To simplify the complex world of Computer Architecture.
  • To showcase Premium Hardware through a curated gallery.
  • To provide a structured Comparison of modern parts.
  • To practice advanced HTML and CSS layout techniques.

3. Project Specifications

Category Details

4. Educational Value

By exploring this site, users will learn the difference between Central Processing Units (CPUs) and Graphics Processing Units (GPUs). We believe that education through visual demonstration is the most effective way to master computer hardware.
